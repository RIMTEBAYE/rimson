Our Services

Agriculture Training

Agriculture is the main source of income for 80% of the world's poor population. This sector therefore plays a decisive role in reducing poverty, increasing income and improving food security.

Breeding training

Raising goats has several advantages for the breeder. It's a ruminant which adapts very easily to various climates, it can be in very diverse environments where other ruminants could not survive; she resists in very arid and dry regions; thus she easily lives in very poor environments because she is satisfied with shrubby vegetation.

Breeding training

Raising chickens allows us to have better control over their health. As soon as a rooster or a hen gets sick, we know it much faster than in other systems. Better egg hygiene by reducing the number of dirty eggs.

soap making

Indeed, the advantage of natural artisanal soap is that it is made with only fatty substances (oils, vegetable butters) moisturizing, repairing and softening, and is often enriched with essential oils and clays, which also have actions for the skin.

jam making

The main advantages of this process lie in low temperatures and short pasteurization times. The gentle processing ensures an optimal end product in terms of appearance, color, taste and retention of nutritional elements.

Sewing training

Training in industrial sewing and development of a production workshop to contribute to the emancipation and professional integration of women in rural areas
